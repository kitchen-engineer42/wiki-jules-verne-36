# Hulda Hansen

## Overview

At the inn of Dal in the Telemark she is "the _froken_, the young lady of the house, as her mother was the madame" (TN-003-017).

To all Norway she is known simply as "Hulda Hansen, of Dal" (TN-013-022).

She is "certainly charming, with the slightly melancholy expression of the daughters of the North" (TN-003-018).

## Role in the Story

In the mountains she insists on rescuing a stranger in peril, crying "some one who is in danger is calling for help" (TN-008-033).

Her sailor's last message reaches her in a bottle: "The 'Viking' is going down. I have only this ticket left of all I hoped to bring back to you" (TN-011-116).

The enclosure proves to be "a lottery ticket bearing the number 9672" (TN-011-113).

As the ticket becomes a national sensation worth a fortune, "Hulda refused all these offers" (TN-012-029).

Ole returns alive and the ticket wins, and at the wedding all see "how beautiful Hulda looked under her bridal crown when she left the little chapel at Dal" (TN-020-025).

## Character & Traits

The narrator calls her "this modest and honest young girl" (TN-005-001), unambitious and content.

Her love for the absent Ole is steadfast, so that "they were united in thought, in spite of the distance that separated them" (TN-001-074).

She would not sell the ticket at any price: "She saw in it only the last farewell of her shipwrecked lover--a memento she wished to reverently preserve" (TN-012-030).

Through the long waiting her devotion never fades: "The poor girl lived only upon the recollection of Ole" (TN-015-050).

## Relationships

- Ole Kamp, the young sailor, is her cousin and betrothed, who "never lagged behind unless it was to keep his cousin Hulda company" (TN-004-006).

- Joel, her brother, is her closest support; in her grief she clings "to her brother as if he was the only friend she had left in the world" (TN-007-014).

- Sylvius Hog, the kindly deputy she and Joel rescued, becomes their champion, feeling "such a paternal affection for the brother and sister" (TN-015-003).
